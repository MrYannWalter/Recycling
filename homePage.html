Different types of waste


Household waste can be classified in 5 different categories: Organic waste, Liquide waste, Solid rubbish, Recyclable waste and Hazardous waste

Liquide Waste

Liquide waste includes dirty water, wash water, organic liquide, waste detergents and sometimes rain water. This waste is usually found in households, bsuinesses and industries.

Organic Waste

Organic waste is also common in almost all households. This waste consists of food waste, garden waste and so on. While this waste is broken down by micro-organisms overtime, it still needs to be appropriately disposed when generated.

Hazardous Waste

Hazard waste refers to any waste that's toxic, flammable, corrosif or reactif. Such waste can be harmful to both you and the environment. Exemples of hazardous waste include toxic chemicals and electronic rubbish.

Solid Waste

Solid rubbish includes a large variety of items that may be found in households or commercial locations. To understand this type of waste better, let's break it down into five categories:

  • Plastic waste: bags, containers, jars, plastic bottles.
  • Paper or card waste: newspapers, packaging materials,cardboard etc.
  • Tins and metals: in different forms throughout your home.
  • Ceramics and glass: broken ceramic cups and plates, glass bottles etc

    • Recyclable Waste

    Recyclable rubbish as the name suggests refers to all waste that can be converted to reusable products. A significant percentage of items in construction waste such as masonry, metal, paper and furniture can be recycled.

    Waste managment process at home


    Your waste managment process is sequenced in these few steps:


    Firstly you will need various trash cans (at least three), which will each contain a specific category of waste.


    Secondly each trash can must be identified for its purpose either by its color or by its name. You can then sort your waste according to the categories mentioned above.


    Lastly you will need a garbage bag in order to facilitate its transportation.
